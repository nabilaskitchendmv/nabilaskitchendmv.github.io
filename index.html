How to Order

  1. Text or WhatsApp your dish name(s), tray size (Half/Medium/Full), and quantity.
  2. Share your event date + time (please order at least 48 hours in advance).
  3. Tell us pickup or delivery + your location (DMV area).
  4. We’ll confirm availability, total, and your pickup/delivery window.
Pickup & Delivery Available. Delivery fees depend on distance from Leesburg.

Tray Sizes

  • Half Tray: ~ 8–10 servings
  • Medium Tray: ~ 12–15 servings
  • Full Tray: ~ 20–25 servings

Serving counts are estimates and can vary depending on the dish and portion size.
