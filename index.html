How to Order

  1. Text or WhatsApp us your dish name(s), tray size (Half/Medium/Full), and quantity.
  2. Share your event date + time (please order at least 48 hours in advance).
  3. Tell us pickup or delivery and your location (DMV area).
  4. We’ll confirm availability, total, and pickup/delivery window.
Pickup & Delivery Available. Delivery fees depend on distance from Leesburg.
